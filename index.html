ABOUT US

BRENT MONTESORRI SCHOOL

Nestled in the heart of New York, Montessori Secondary School offers a transformative education rooted in student-centered learning. Emphasizing independence, critical thinking, and collaborative problem-solving, the school cultivates an environment where students take ownership of their educational journey. With a curriculum that blends academic rigor with real-world application, students engage in interdisciplinary projects, internships, and community service. The school's small class sizes foster personalized attention, ensuring each student thrives. Through its unique approach, Montessori Secondary School prepares students not only for college but for life, equipping them with the skills and mindset needed to navigate an ever-changing world.

OUR FACILITIES

SERVICES

Personalized Learning

Montessori schools prioritize individualized education, tailoring lessons to each student's pace, interests, and learning style. This approach fosters self-direction, allowing students to explore subjects deeply and develop a love for learning.

Multi-Age Classrooms

In a Montessori setting, students of different ages learn together in a mixed-age environment. This structure promotes mentorship, as older students guide younger ones, reinforcing their own knowledge while fostering leadership skills

Hands-On Learning

Montessori schools emphasize experiential, hands-on learning through tactile materials that engage students actively in the learning process.
